## <i class="fa-solid fa-flask"></i> Research

### NSF Computational Linguistics Group &mdash; UPRM
**Research Assistant, HASPNeL Project** | Mayag&uuml;ez, PR | Jan 2021 &ndash; Present

Developing a cognitively plausible neural-symbolic parser for syntactic disambiguation in Minimalist Grammar, in collaboration with [Sandiway Fong](https://www.cs.arizona.edu/person/sandiway-fong) at the University of Arizona. This work bridges formal linguistics and neural computation, exploring how symbolic grammatical constraints can be integrated with learned representations.

Published at **ICAART 2026**: "A Cognitively Plausible Neural-Symbolic Model for Syntactic Disambiguation in Minimalist Grammar."

<a href="https://www.uprm.edu/portada/2022/12/16/nsf-computacion/" target="_blank">Featured in UPRM's NSF computing research article &rarr;</a>

### Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory
**AI Researcher** &mdash; AI, Neuroscience, and Complex Systems Group | Laurel, MD | May 2025 &ndash; Present

Developing a multimodal speech-decoding framework fusing video, audio, and neural signals to support assistive communication for speakers with dysarthria. Conducted modality ablation studies, improving Phoneme Error Rate by 12% and Word Error Rate by 11% over baseline. Also architected an agentic laboratory system using Vision-Language Models to orchestrate visual inspection and automate experimental decision-making.

### MIT Lincoln Laboratory
**Research Co-op** &mdash; AI Technology and Systems Group | Lexington, MA | May 2022 &ndash; Dec 2024

Published at **NAACL 2025**: ["Masks and Mimicry: Strategic Obfuscation and Impersonation Attacks on Authorship Verification."](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.19099)
