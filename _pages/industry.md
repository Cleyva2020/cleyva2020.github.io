### <img src="/assets/img/logos/nsf.svg" alt="NSF" style="height: 28px; vertical-align: middle;"> NLP / Computational Linguistics Group @ UPRM
**Research Assistant** | Jan 2021 &ndash; Present

Developing a cognitively plausible neural-symbolic parser for syntactic disambiguation in Minimalist Grammar, in collaboration with [Sandiway Fong](https://www.cs.arizona.edu/person/sandiway-fong) at the University of Arizona. This work bridges formal linguistics and neural computation, exploring how symbolic grammatical constraints can be integrated with learned representations.

Published at **ICAART 2026**: ["A Cognitively Plausible Neural-Symbolic Model for Syntactic Disambiguation in Minimalist Grammar."](https://doi.org/10.5220/0014481700004052)

### <img src="/assets/img/logos/jhuapl.svg" alt="JHU APL" style="height: 28px; vertical-align: middle;"> Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory
**AI Researcher** &mdash; AI, Neuroscience, and Complex Systems Group | May 2025 &ndash; Present

Developing a multimodal speech-decoding framework building on [AV-HuBERT](https://github.com/facebookresearch/av_hubert), fusing video, audio, and neural signals to support assistive communication for speakers with dysarthria.
